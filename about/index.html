A bit about myself.

Born in Vancouver in 1996, I grew up on the east side of the city. I attended Sir Guy Carleton Elementary from 2001 to 2009, and Killarney Secondary from 2009 to 2014. From there, I studied chemistry at Simon Fraser University for one year before transferring to the University of British Columbia, where I declared my Computer Engineering major in 2016.

The first time I started coding was in 2012. In 2013 I worked with a classmate to remodel my high school's website. The following year I decided to take the Canadian Computing Competition which honestly, made me feel a little less smart. Nonetheless, it was a learning experience for me. At my high school graduation, I was awarded a district scholarship for information technology. Despite originally choosing to pursue a chemsitry degree, my passion for programming never died, so here I am, pursing my Bachelor of Applied Science in Computer Engineering.
