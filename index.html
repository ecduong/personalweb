Hi, my name is Eric Duong. I am a third year computer engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I am also a teaching assistant for the computer science department and an active member of the UBC Rocket engineering student design team.

Things I do

UBC Rocket

I am an avionics team member on the UBC Rocket engineering student design team. Currently, I am working on a live telemetry unit for the rocket, which will allow us to track and map the rocket's whereabouts while it is in flight.

Previously, I worked on Cypress' payload parachute deployment system. Cypress placed first in the 10,000 ft commercial off the shelf category at the 2017 Spaceport America Cup with a recorded apogee of 10,053 ft.
